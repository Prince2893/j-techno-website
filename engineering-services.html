Mechanical Design & Engineering

Detailed engineering design with manufacturing considerations integrated from the start.

Design Standards:
DIN/ISO Standards GD&T DFM/DFA

Design Capabilities

  • 3D CAD Design: Professional modeling using Autodesk Inventor, SolidWorks
  • Manufacturing-Ready 2D Drawings: Complete production drawings with tolerances
  • Weldment and Sheet-Metal Design: Specialized design for fabrication processes
  • Special Purpose Machines (SPM): Custom machinery design for specific applications

Simulation & Design Validation

Validate designs before manufacturing to ensure performance, reliability, and safety.

Analysis Methods:
FEA Kinematics Tolerance Stack-up

Validation Services

  • Structural Analysis (FEA): Stress, strain, and deformation analysis
  • Thermal Analysis: Heat transfer and thermal stress evaluation
  • Fatigue Analysis: Life prediction under cyclic loading conditions
  • Motion & Mechanism Simulation: Kinematic and dynamic analysis
  • Tolerance Analysis: Statistical tolerance stack-up analysis
  • Design Optimization: Iterative improvement based on simulation results

Simulation reduces prototyping costs and prevents field failures.
