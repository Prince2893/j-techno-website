Research & Innovation in Handling & Automation

We focus exclusively on research, concept design, simulation, and development of handling systems — manipulators, grippers, robotic systems with sensors and 3D vision, AGVs, and IoT-enabled automation. We do not engage in mass production; our strength is engineering precision and bespoke R&D for German and EU clients.

Manipulators

Development Process

  1. Research & Concept: Requirements & feasibility.
  2. Design & Simulation: CAD, motion study, control architectures.
  3. Prototype & Integration: Lab verification, sensor/vision integration.
  4. Delivery: Technical documentation, control interfaces & handover.

Note: We provide design, prototypes and integration support — manufacturing/volume production is outside our scope.
