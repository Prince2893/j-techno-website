Research & Innovation in Handling & Automation

Our dedicated R&D team focuses exclusively on concept design, simulation, and development of advanced handling systems — manipulators, robotic vision, AGVs, and IoT integration. We deliver engineering precision and bespoke R&D for German and EU clients.

Manipulators

Design of semi-automatic and fully automatic manipulators for ergonomic, safe, and repeatable handling tasks. We deliver concept designs, load & reach studies, and motion simulations.

Advanced Grippers

Adaptive electric, pneumatic, and soft gripper concepts for mixed-material handling. Focus on force control, compliance, and integration with robot controllers.

Robotic Systems (Sensors & Vision)

Robots integrated with 3D vision cameras, sensors, and real-time analytics. Solutions include part recognition, error detection, and closed-loop control for high precision tasks.

Automated Guided Vehicles (AGVs)

Modular AGV concepts for intralogistics: navigation strategies, payload interfaces, and system integration for optimized material flow.

IoT & Smart Systems

Edge-to-cloud connectivity, sensor networks, and dashboard prototypes for monitoring KPIs, predictive maintenance, and Industry 4.0 readiness.

Development Process

  1. Research & Concept: Requirements & feasibility.
  2. Design & Simulation: CAD, motion study, control architectures.
  3. Prototype & Integration: Lab verification, sensor/vision integration.
  4. Delivery: Technical documentation, control interfaces & handover.

Note: We provide design, prototypes, and integration support. Manufacturing/volume production is outside our R&D scope.
