02The Product

CE DocSuite — Complete CE Documentation, Generated Automatically

Every document required for CE marking of your machine or automation system — produced in hours, not weeks.

Betriebsanleitung (Operating Instructions)

Machine-specific operating manual per IEC/IEEE 82079-1 and DIN EN ISO 20607. German and English.

Risikobeurteilung (Risk Assessment)

Systematic hazard identification and risk estimation per ISO 12100 and Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC.

Konformitätserklärung (Declaration of Conformity)

EU Declaration of Conformity with all applicable directives and harmonised standards listed automatically.

Technische Dokumentation (Technical File – Annex VII)

Complete technical construction file index structured for Notified Body review.

Sicherheitskonzept (Safety Concept)

Safety architecture documentation per ISO 13849 with Performance Level determination.

Wartungs- und Prüfplan (Maintenance Plan)

Component-specific maintenance schedules and inspection intervals.

Validation

Design Validation Process

Every design undergoes systematic validation: concept feasibility review, 3D design review for manufacturing feasibility (DFM), tolerance stack-up analysis, FEA for structural integrity, and motion simulation for mechanism validation. Deliverables are checked against customer specifications and relevant standards (DIN, ISO) before handover. Design iterations are documented, and lessons learned are integrated into future projects.
