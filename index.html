Automation Products & Industrial Components

A complete portfolio of mobile robots, industrial robots, vision systems, conveyors, safety systems, and precision mechanical components – supporting full factory and warehouse automation for German & EU industries.

Mobile Robotics (AGVs & AMRs)

Autonomous mobile platforms for intralogistics, line feeding, pallet transport and warehouse material flow.

  • Tugger, Forklift & Pallet AGVs
  • Transport, Shelf/Cart & Pallet AMRs
  • Robot arm–mounted AMRs
  • Cleaning, inspection & telepresence robots

Industrial Robots

High-performance robotic systems for handling, assembly, welding, packaging and precision operations.

  • 6-axis & 7-axis articulated robots
  • SCARA robots (high-speed & heavy-load)
  • Delta pick-and-place robots
  • Cartesian/gantry robots
  • Collaborative robots (desktop to heavy payload)

End-of-Arm Tooling (EOAT)

Complete tooling solutions for gripping, welding, dispensing, cutting and automated fastening.

  • Mechanical, pneumatic, soft, magnetic & vacuum grippers
  • Welding guns & torch holders
  • Screwdriving tools & spindles
  • Dispensing & gluing heads
  • Cutting & trimming tools

Vision & Sensing Systems

Smart 2D/3D vision and sensors for positioning, guidance, inspection and intelligent automation.

  • 2D vision cameras & smart cameras
  • 3D vision systems & depth sensors
  • LiDAR & proximity sensors
  • Force–torque sensors
  • Barcode & RFID scanners

Conveying & Material Transport

Material flow solutions connecting receiving, production, packaging and dispatch.

  • Belt, roller & chain conveyors
  • Modular plastic conveyors
  • Overhead conveyors & transfer modules
  • Turntables, lift units & diverts
  • Pallet conveyor systems

Storage & Retrieval Automation

High-density automated storage solutions for warehouses and production supermarkets.

  • Unit-load & mini-load AS/RS
  • Shuttle & multi-shuttle systems
  • Mobile racking systems
  • Vertical lift modules (VLM)
  • Vertical carousels & robotic storage walls

Machine Tending & Workstations

Automated loading, unloading and feeding solutions for CNC and assembly processes.

  • Robotic machine tending cells
  • Robotic part-feeding systems
  • CNC loading AMR/AGV cells
  • Automatic tool changers

Packaging & Palletizing Systems

End-of-line automation for carton forming, packing, palletizing and wrapping.

  • Robotic palletizers & depalletizers
  • Case packers & box erectors
  • Layer forming and pallet handling
  • Automatic stretch wrapping machines

Inspection & Quality Automation

  • Vision inspection systems

Digital Automation & Control
