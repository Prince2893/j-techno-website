ProductCE DocSuite

CE Compliance Documentation, Generated From Your Machine Data

CE DocSuite is an AI-assisted documentation tool that drafts CE-compliant machinery documents from the technical details of your machine, with mandatory engineer review before anything is generated.

01What's Included

Three Document Types Live Today, Three More on the Roadmap

We'd rather tell you plainly what's ready to use today than oversell what's still on the roadmap.

Live Today

Betriebsanleitung

Operating instructions structured to DIN EN 82079-1 — safety warnings, LOTO procedures, PPE requirements, and maintenance tasks, in German and English.

Live Today

Konformitätserklärung

EU Declaration of Conformity per Annex II, No. 1, Letter A of the Machinery Directive 2006/42/EC — manufacturer identity, applicable directives, and signatory details.

Live Today

Risikobeurteilung

Risk assessment per EN ISO 12100 — hazard identification, initial and residual risk scoring, protective measures, and a defensibility summary.

Roadmap

Technische Dokumentation

The technical file structure that backs up a Declaration of Conformity. Not yet available.

Roadmap

Sicherheitskonzept

A structured machine safety concept document. Not yet available.

Roadmap

Wartungs- und Prüfplan

A structured maintenance and inspection plan. Not yet available.

Founding Member plans lock in access to roadmap document types the moment each one ships — see Pricing for details.

02How It Works

From Machine Details to a Downloadable Document

STEP 01

Fill in Machine Details

Describe your machine or system — components, hazard zones, life phases, and technical specifications.

STEP 02

AI Drafts the Document

AI proposes hazard descriptions, measures, and document text from what you've entered — every suggestion is flagged until reviewed.

STEP 03

You Review & Generate

Accept, edit, or reject every AI suggestion. Nothing reaches the final document without your sign-off.

STEP 04

Download

Get a real Word document — formatted and numbered, ready to hand to your compliance function.
